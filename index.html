An Overview

Jheel
International School With Greate Facility

Our School in Karanja Lad was founded with the goal of igniting children's imaginations. Our ongoing goal is to provide children with a well-rounded education and interesting curriculum with a goal and focus on social emotional development as well as a deep understanding of the importance of early academic education. Our preschool provides a masterfully balanced environment of love, learning, and leadership that fosters curiosity and a desire to learn.

Our main goal in school is to engage children in an early learning setting that honours both the kid and the enchantment of childhood, as well as to pique their curiosity and prepare them for the GLOBAL WORLD OF TOMORROW.
